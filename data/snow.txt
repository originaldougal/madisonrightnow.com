        <h1>Declared Snow Emergency Rules</h1>
        <ul>
            <li>A Declared Snow Emergency is when snow accumulates to 3" or  more and all the streets in the downtown <a href="SEZ.cfm">Snow Emergency Zone</a> must be  plowed. </li>
            <li><strong>When this occurs, people who park in the Snow Emergency Zone must  abide by the <a href="/residents/Winter/Parking/alternateSideParking.cfm">alternate side parking rules </a> for a minimum of the next two nights.</strong></li>
            <li>A Snow Emergency is declared whenever it becomes necessary to plow ALL residential streets. </li>
            <li>A Snow Emergency will always be declared prior to 9 p.m. if it is to take effect that night.</li>
            <li>If conditions warrant, a Snow Emergency can be extended beyond the 48 hours.</li>
            <li>A Snow Emergency is a City-wide policy. </li>
        </ul>
        <p><strong>Please note</strong>: <em>During a Snow Emergency, if you violate the Alternate Side Parking rules, the fines  are $60, plus a $65 towing fee.</em></p>
